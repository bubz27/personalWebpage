Mōku/About Me

I am a graduated teacher seeking to expand my employment opportunities through a career in the technology industry. In the long term I see myself combining my teaching skills and experience gained in the technology sector to enhance the lives of Māori, Pasifika and other communities through technology and education. I entered the field of education because I believe it to be one of the most powerful vehicles for improving opportunities, thus improving success. I believe education to be the proverbial ‘ambulance’, - except at the top of the cliff. I have witnessed the dramatic rise of the technology industry and see my future career path here. I plan to secure employment and gain experience in this industry for at least the next decade. Beyond that, I have a vision to combine this experience with my teaching background in order to upskill those whom this industry mightn’t be accessible to. This comes from my belief that enabling equitable outcomes can start from myself, within my own home and that we each have a role to play in securing the futures of those in vulnerable environments.
